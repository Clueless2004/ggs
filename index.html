SDG: Life on Land

Life on Land (SDG 15) aims to protect, restore, and promote the sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems. Our projects focus on preserving biodiversity, preventing deforestation, and restoring degraded lands. Together, we can ensure a better future for all living organisms and protect the planet's natural resources.
